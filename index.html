Saint Joseph's University Basketball

2020-21 Season

The Hawks would start the 20-21 campaign with a young head coach( Billy Lange), a group of role players and one determined All American Ryan Daly.

The seasons prospects didn't look very bright coming off a 6-26 season the year before

Billy Lange

Coach Facts

  • Member of Coaching at Villinova for 5 Years
  • Patriot League Coach of The Year in 2008

Ryan Daly

Ryan Daly Facts

  • Scored Over 1,000 Points at The University of Delaware Before Tranfering to Hawk Hill
  • Averaged 18.5 Points, 6.0 Rebounds, and 2.5 Assists in the Hawks 2020-21 Season
  • Led Hawks Past La Salle In the Atlantc 10 Tournament Finishing With 23 Points
